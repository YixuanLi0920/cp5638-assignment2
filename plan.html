Project Plan:

Goals

We hope to improve the business of the Petite Treats bakery through the internet, let more people know about the Petite Treats bakery through the website, and attract more customers, so as to improve the turnover and profit. And let users access to product information easily.

Success Evaluation

Two months after the website was launched, the number of people coming to the bakery increased significantly,
or the turnover increased by more than 20%.

Target Audience

Target audience are young families and young people, possibly around the age of 17 to 35.
